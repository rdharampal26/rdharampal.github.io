Unit 1 & Unit 2: Key Concepts

In Unit 1, we learned about binary systems, where data is represented using only two symbols: 0 and 1. We also explored the difference between lossy and lossless compression techniques. While lossy compression reduces file sizes by discarding some data, lossless compression retains all original data for perfect quality.

Unit 2 introduced the concept of networks and routers, showing how the internet works. Networks are systems of interconnected devices, and routers act as the traffic controllers, directing data to its destination. This section helped me understand the structure of the internet and how information flows across the world.
